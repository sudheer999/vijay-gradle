Why do I have to under go a probationary period? How will it effect my terms of employment?
The probationary period is the initial period of employment, a kind of ?trial period?, during which the company carefully considers whether the employee is able to meet the standards and expectations of the job and if the employee should be retained.

The probationary period is applicable to all our employees, irrespective of their previous experience or designation on joining Diksha. This period will be used to assess not only your technical capabilities but also on how you are able to adjust to the work environment and culture of Diksha Technologies.

On satisfactory performance during probationary period, HR-SPOC shall confirm the services by sending a soft copy of Confirmation letter; Reporting Manager/Manager HR reserves the right to extend this period of probation for an additional specified period by notifying the same through an email.

In terms of salary, working time regulations, insurance etc. you are entitled to the same protection during your probationary period as are the permanent members of staff. However, while you are on probation you are entitled to one day leave per month.

What are the Sodexo meal vouchers? How will I benefit from them?
A Sodexo Meal Voucher is a non-transferable instrument, which can be used across 15,000 outlets across India, to pay for food and Beverages at the affiliated outlets.

Sodexo Vouchers provides you with an opportunity to save on tax. The entire amount spent through the Sodexo Meal Vouchers is exempt from Income Tax. In simple terms, if you pay for meals, snacks, food & beverages through the Sodexo Cafeteria Vouchers it will cost you up to 33.99% less. At Rs.2200 per month, you will save upto Rs 8,800 per annum in taxes without making any investments.

Why are some of my claims rejected?
Please find the benefits that are included and excluded in the policy listed below. Only claims that are covered by the policy shall be reimbursed.

Included Benefits:

1) The policy reimburses the hospital expenses incurred for a minimum period of 24 hours of hospitalisation due to sickness or Accident for a complete sum insured in one hospitalisation or a series of hospitalisation in one year.
2) 30 days pre hospitalisation and 60 days post hospitalisation is covered in the policy.
3) Specific day care surgeries like cataract, endoscopy, colonoscopy, dialysis, chemotherapy are also covered which do not require 24hrs hospitalisation.
4) The policy will cover room rent, nursing charges, operation theatre charges, surgeon fees, anaesthetist fees, medicines, lab charges, diagnostic charges, consultation fees, ambulance charges. The room rent will be restricted to Rs.1000/- per day.
5) In case of non network hospital, the hospital must have at least 15 beds with all facilities.


Excluded Benefits:

1) The policy does not cover any outpatient treatments like cough, cold, fever...etc which are taken on day to day basis.
2) The policy does not cover dental treatments, cosmetic surgeries, congenital diseases, Ayurvedic treatments, homeopathy naturopathy and conventional method of treatments.
3) The policy will not cover maternity, abortions, family planning procedures like vasectomy, tubectomy and other related surgeries.
4) The policy will not cover food expenses, entertainment expenses, telephone expenses, family member expenses during the stay in the hospital.
5) Self inflicted hospitalisations like suicide, alcohol, war, assault, murder, violation of law are not covered in the policy.
6) Hospitalisations which are purely diagnostic in nature will not be covered.
7) Cost of spectacles, dentures, lens, laser treatment for correction of vision, hearing aids, walkers, wheel chairs, walking sticks are all not covered in the policy during the hospitalisation or after discharge.

How do we claim for reimbursements under Insurance Policy?
1) In case of listed hospital -A Cashless facility will be extended only in the network of hospitals. At the time of hospitalization the health card that is issued at the time of joining has to be produced to hospital. Paramount Health Services settles the bills directly to the hospitals.

2) In case of non-network hospital - In case the treatment is taken in a non network hospital, all the required documents like the claim form, discharge summary, bills, cash memos, lab reports, scanning reports have to be duly signed and sealed by the hospital should be produced to your respective HR SPOC; the bills have to be submitted within 15 days from the date of discharge from the hospital. HR SPOC will send the claims to Paramount Health Services and gets it reimbursed.
